Portfolio detail

Hydrogel Simulator is a compelling project showcased in my portfolio, designed to provide a user-friendly and accessible experience for individuals seeking a better understanding of hydrogel materials. This simulator offers an interactive platform where users can explore the properties and behavior of hydrogels in a virtual environment. Through meticulous coding and development, I have transformed this project into a web-based application, allowing for easy access and utilization by a diverse range of users. By publishing the simulator on a website, I have ensured that individuals can conveniently engage with the application, fostering a deeper understanding of the unique characteristics and applications of hydrogels.
